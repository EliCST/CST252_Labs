Sides In A Shape!

A fun educational game that explores the many sides that are in shapes. The shape on screen is clickable and tells the player how many sides are in a shape. Once the shape is clicked, another side is added until the player hits a dodecahedron is formed.

I want my project to be an interactive clickable game. Sides in a Shape (working title) is an interactive game designed to educate people the different types of shapes with the sides they have in them.

As the player places the mouse cursor over a shape, a text window will appear next to the shape, telling the player the name of the shape as well as how many sides are in it.

The interactivity of this game comes from clicking on the shape. As the player uses the mouse to click on a shape, it gains another side. For example, once the clicks on a triangle, another side forms and it becomes a square. Clicking on the square adds another side, the result being the square becomes a pentagon. And so on and so forth.
